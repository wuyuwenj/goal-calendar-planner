- **Font:** SF Pro Display Bold (or system bold)

- **Line Height:** 1.1
- **Alignment:** Center

- **Font:** SF Pro Text Regular
- **Size:** 36-42px

- **Line Height:** 1.3
- **Alignment:** Center
